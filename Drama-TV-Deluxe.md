# DRAMA TV DELUXE — THE DRAMMYS

The channel's red-carpet, gold-plated **season finale**. Not another episode —
an awards gala where the whole universe shows up at once: spotlights sweep the
red carpet, a confetti cannon fires over the winner, the room is FLOORED in a
standing ovation, the acceptance speech gets struck by lightning, and then the
finale crashes the ceremony — the duck takes the stage, and **THE LEVEL** hands
out its final award. To you. By name.

Seven acts:

1. **RED CARPET** — spotlights, a gossip chyron, the stars arrive
2. **PREVIOUSLY, ON EVERYTHING** — a lightning recap of the whole canon
3. **THE NOMINEES** — the category, and five gloriously petty nominees
4. **THE ENVELOPE** — a golden envelope, a drumroll, a held breath
5. **AND THE DRAMMY GOES TO…** — the WINNER, the trophy, the CONFETTI CANNON, an ovation that FLOORS the room
6. **THE SPEECH** — tearful, overwrought, struck by lightning, then a twist
7. **THE FINALE** — the duck presents THE LEVEL's final award to **YOU**; gold credits roll
