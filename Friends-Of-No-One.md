# FRIENDS OF NO ONE

DRAMA TV presents an **emotional hostage situation**, staged in your terminal.
Conference Room B. The fluorescent lights flicker. Everyone is holding everyone
else emotionally hostage, and the demands are things like *ACKNOWLEDGE MY
FEELINGS* and *CC ME ON EVERYTHING*. A weary HR negotiator works the room while
a spiralling syllogism crawls across the screen —

> A friend to all is a friend to no one.
> So a friend of no one is a friend of everyone.
> He said, she said: none-friend = all-friend,
> so he said, she said: all-friend = none-friend…

— accelerating and tightening until it eats its own tail. Then **THE REVEAL**
completes the logic, the room is **FLOORED**… and the cliffhanger lands:

> **…but they pay you. :3**

The paycheck flutters down, everyone sits back down, the door was never locked,
and the meeting is adjourned — *recurring every Tuesday.*

The beats:

1. **EMOTIONAL HOSTAGE SITUATION — CONFERENCE ROOM B**
2. **THE STANDOFF** — the table, the hostage faces, the flickering lights
3. **THE DEMAND** — an escalating emotional demand
4. **THE PARADOX** — the syllogism spirals, faster and faster
5. **THE REVEAL** — *…hence: you are all each other's hostage* — FLOORED
6. **…BUT THEY PAY YOU :3** — a paycheck saves everyone
7. **MEETING ADJOURNED… AND RECURRING** — fade, then static

```powershell
.\Friends-Of-No-One.ps1                       # the meeting, forever -- press any key to leave (you can't, but try)
.\Friends-Of-No-One.ps1 -Meetings 2           # two standoffs, then off-air
.\Friends-Of-No-One.ps1 -Calm                 # a functional workplace: feelings acknowledged, meeting actually ends
.\Friends-Of-No-One.ps1 -Fast                 # snappier holds (for the impatient)
.\Friends-Of-No-One.ps1 -Silent               # no beeps (a tense, quiet open-plan)
.\Friends-Of-No-One.ps1 -Storyboard -Scenes 1 -Seed 42   # the static montage above
```
