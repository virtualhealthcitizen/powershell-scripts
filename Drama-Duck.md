# Drama duck

A one-duck melodrama staged in your terminal: the curtain rises, a spotlight
finds a deeply theatrical duck, and it weeps its way through a soliloquy, drops
a scandalous confession on a flash of lightning, swoons onto the fainting couch,
and takes a tearful bow to a rose-throwing standing ovation. Then it does it
all again.

```powershell
.\Drama-Duck.ps1                       # curtain up, forever -- press any key to bring the house lights up
.\Drama-Duck.ps1 -Acts 3               # three full tragedies, then the theatre goes dark
.\Drama-Duck.ps1 -Silent               # no beeps (a silent matinee)
.\Drama-Duck.ps1 -Storyboard -Scenes 1 -Seed 42   # the static montage above
```
