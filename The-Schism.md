# THE SCHISM

DRAMA TV's late-night **televangelist melodrama**, staged in your terminal. A
charismatic founder has had ENOUGH — *that does it* — and founds a church:

> **THE CHURCH OF "THAT DOES IT, YOU HAD BETTER NOT IMAGINE THAT."**

From the pulpit comes the threat: imagine it, and ELSE — he will imagine what
will happen, *after* you have imagined. Then his friends storm off to found their
own rival churches (including one where they imagine what happened *before* you
imagined), and **THE PROPHECY** spirals — imagining what was imagined before it
will have been imagined — until it eats its own tail:

> I imagine what will happen. I imagine what happens after you imagined. They
> imagine what happened before you imagined… which is, at this point, **a church.**

**THE REVEAL**: every church was the same church. The congregation is **FLOORED**.
And the founder takes two months off. For no reason. *It is foretold.*

The beats:

1. **THAT DOES IT** — a founder has had enough
2. **THE FOUNDING** — the church is named, verbatim; the marquee blazes
3. **THE FIRST SERMON** — the threat from the pulpit
4. **THE SCHISM** — friends storm off; rival church signs multiply
5. **THE PROPHECY** — the recursive imagination spirals and tightens
6. **THE REVEAL** — *every church was the same church* — FLOORED
7. **THE TWO-MONTH SABBATICAL** — he leaves, for no reason, foretold (the prophecy names *you* as a founding member)
8. **SERVICE CONCLUDED** — fade to a reverent static

```powershell
.\The-Schism.ps1                       # service, forever -- press any key to leave the pew
.\The-Schism.ps1 -Services 2           # two sermons, then the doors close
.\The-Schism.ps1 -Calm                 # an ecumenical ending: the schisms reconcile, churches merge
.\The-Schism.ps1 -Fast                 # snappier holds (for the impatient)
.\The-Schism.ps1 -Silent               # no beeps (a silent vigil)
.\The-Schism.ps1 -Storyboard -Scenes 1 -Seed 42   # the static montage above
```
