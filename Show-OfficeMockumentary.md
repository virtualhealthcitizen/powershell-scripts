# The open plan

"THE OPEN PLAN" -- an ASCII office mockumentary that plays in your terminal. A
fake TV broadcast complete with a blinking REC light, running timecode, and
lower-third subtitles, where the staff break into deadpan "talking head"
confessional cutaways roughly every ten seconds. Snark included at no extra
charge.

```powershell
.\Show-OfficeMockumentary.ps1                # play one episode at documentary pace
.\Show-OfficeMockumentary.ps1 -Fast          # snappier typing, shorter dramatic pauses
.\Show-OfficeMockumentary.ps1 -Forever       # loop the episode until Ctrl+C, like corporate life
.\Show-OfficeMockumentary.ps1 -Fast -Forever # the binge-watch
```
