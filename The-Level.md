# THE LEVEL

DRAMA TV's haunted game show, staged in your terminal. A too-smooth host, a
sweating contestant from somewhere like Diss, a live studio crowd, and ONE RULE
that blinks in red between every round:

> **UNDER NO CIRCUMSTANCES PUT THE LEVEL BACK IN THE LEVEL.**

They bank a few safe answers (the crowd claps, then rises), the host dangles a
double-or-nothing jackpot... and then, inevitably, they put THE LEVEL back in
THE LEVEL. The board folds into itself -- THE LEVEL inside THE LEVEL inside THE
LEVEL, boxes within boxes to the vanishing point -- and the room is not just
FLOORED. It is **FLOORED, FLOORED**: a double slam. Floor the house enough times
and THE LEVEL stops pretending it was ever a show, and looks back at you by name.

(Canon with the dread that haunts `Drama-TV.ps1`.)

```powershell
.\The-Level.ps1                       # broadcasts forever, press any key to kill the feed
.\The-Level.ps1 -Rounds 3             # three rounds then off-air
.\The-Level.ps1 -Calm                 # nobody breaks the rule; a cosy quiz night, a winner!
.\The-Level.ps1 -Haunt                # straight to: THE LEVEL looks back
.\The-Level.ps1 -Silent               # no beeps (a tense, quiet studio)
.\The-Level.ps1 -Storyboard -Scenes 1 -Seed 42   # the static montage above
```
