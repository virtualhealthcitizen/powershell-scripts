# Floored

A one-liner staged in your terminal: open on a flat, unbothered ASCII talking
head, he sets it up, then deadpans one devastating line dead into the camera --
ba-dum-tss -- and holds the world's longest beat of silence. Then the audience
is FLOORED: jaws drop, hands fly up, the APPLAUSE meter pins, the FLOORED card
detonates, the room shakes, and at full burn a chair gets thrown. Then he does
it all again.

```powershell
.\Floored.ps1                       # open mic, forever -- press Ctrl+C to bring the house lights up
.\Floored.ps1 -Bits 5               # five bits, then he leaves the building
.\Floored.ps1 -Fast                 # type faster, shorter holds (for the impatient)
.\Floored.ps1 -Silent               # no beeps (a tough room)
.\Floored.ps1 -Storyboard -Scenes 2 -Seed 42   # the static montage above
```
